Hey, welcome to Good Taste! I’m Rachel Jordan and I made this website in 2018 for the Dev Catalyst Competition. The contest required one web portfolio and I was running out of time to meet the deadline, so I made this website in a few days. It was barely functional back then, but looked alright to be last-minute. Since then, it’s simply made a bad taste in my mouth, so I began to compile a list of websites that I actually think are cool and leave a good taste instead. I've also included links to some of my older websites as well. Take your time to check out every one!
